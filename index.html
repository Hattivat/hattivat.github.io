About me

My name is Jan Janiczek, and I hold a combined "human languages plus computer science fundamentals" (Linguistics and Information Science) BA degree from the Adam Mickiewicz University in Poznań. Following graduation, I spent two years as a freelance English-Polish translator, culminating in the publication of an RPG sourcebook entirely translated by me. Disappointed by the career prospects of, and perhaps more importantly, the lack of employer respect for, translators in my country, I have decided to make use of the Computer-Science-related part of my degree. I have joined the Access Management team in the IT Security Support division of GlaxoSmithKline as a trainee, and spent 18 months working there, putting into practical use my knowledge of SQL and Python, and learning to use PowerShell and Active Directory. Although I enjoyed working in the multi-national environment of GSK, when my apprenticeship ended I decided to take time off work to further develop my skills and become a programmer.
